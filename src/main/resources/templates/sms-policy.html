SMS Notification Policy

Service: SKT Scheduler

Types of Messages
  • New schedule posted
  • Schedule updates
  • Shift changes
Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on scheduling activity.

Opt-In

Employees opt in during onboarding by providing their mobile phone number and verbally agreeing to receive work-related SMS notifications about schedules and shift updates.

Opt-Out

Employees can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Fees

Message and data rates may apply.
